The Networks

Both water and power are brought to us in a simple yet frequently over looked system. They are both comodities that have to be brought into peoples living spaces from a larger sorce. Both are transported underground and into homes where they can be used for our everyday needs. The internet is much the same but we dont think of it that way because it feels less tangable. The internet is just another utility that has to be brought to places where people live and into their homes so they can access it.
