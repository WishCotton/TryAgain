Water

Water is yet another infrastructure that we pay very little attention to though we use it everyday. In many ways the structure required to get this water to us is very similar to the structure required to get electricity to us. Both have to be brought into areas wear people live from a sorce and in both these examples the electricity and the water move underground and into peoples homes.
