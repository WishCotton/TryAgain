Unseen Infrastructures

Most of us take it as a given that when we flip a switch a light will go on, or that if we turn a tap water will come out. Our day to day lives rely on being able to use power, water and in most cases the internet. These are some of the most crucial parts of our lives but we rarely think about the infrastructures required for us to all have acsses to these neccesities. There are unseen networks all around us.

I want to take a closer look at a few of these systems we all use and explore the similarities between them. Many of there networks seem to run along similar pathways and follow similar patterns.
